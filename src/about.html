Welcome/About

Let's start with one of my favorite quote, "Life is an ongoing process and journey of self discovery".

Hi there, My name is Enoch Kweku Adaboh - just a normal guy trying to live and not to make a living. I am a Front-End Developer and an IT Technician. My career did not begin working with computers. I started as a High School Mathematics teacher back in my home country Ghana. However, life processes happened and I discovery a true passion for computers and working with it. I got my first personal laptop in my third year undergraduate studies at the University of Cape-Coast, Ghana so invariably am a late comer into IT but that has never been an obstacle towards this jorney. I developed and continue to develop my IT technicall skills and knowledge through perservarance, hard work and self-teaching myself.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, watching live soccer games especially games involving my team Liverpool football club and listening to podcasts. I work remote and full-time with Insight Inc currently as a Tier II Support Technician.

I put my heart into anything I do and I look forward to new chanlleging opportunities. The story has just began, Get in Touch and let's add more chapters to it.
